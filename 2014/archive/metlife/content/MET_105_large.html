In 2007, the MetLife Auto and Home Group created a campaign titled “Covering all the IFS of Driving.” It was designed to expand MetLife’s auto business. It was targeted to the employees of MetLife’s institutional customers. MetLife asked MKP to help expand this campaign to direct mail and online communications, leveraging the look and feel of the existing campaign.
