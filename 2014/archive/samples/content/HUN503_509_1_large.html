Huntington Reg E Customer Consent Direct Mail Program
  • Previous

Beyond compliance with new federal regulations, Huntington wanted to obtain the maximum number of opt-in responses in order to stem the loss of overdraft fee income. MKP developed a comprehensive communications plan including a series of response-driven mailings to impacted customers. Five mail packages conveyed an increasingly urgent need to respond so as to prevent interruption of overdraft service. The continuity campaign – supported by online elements, secure emails and branch/ATM signage – delivered the bank’s brand, creating a positive, pro-customer message that was extremely successful in garnering outstanding results.
