To attract a younger customer base, Burke & Herbert Bank, a 21-branch bank in Northern Virginia, introduced two new convenience-oriented checking accounts that offered free access to any ATM nationwide and rewards for every debit card purchase. MKP created an integrated marketing and advertising campaign, the first ever implemented at the bank, including in-branch materials, statement inserts, online content for the bankâ€™s website (banner ads and landing page) and a print and radio advertising campaign. A direct mail campaign to prospects consisted of a self-mailer and a follow-up postcard to the same recipients one month later. All elements of the campaign utilized the same image and main headline to create synergy across all delivery channels. The campaign was hugely successful in generating a substantial lift in new checking relationships and creating excitement among branch sales employees.
