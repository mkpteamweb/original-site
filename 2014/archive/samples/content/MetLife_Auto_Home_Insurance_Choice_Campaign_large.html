As employers continue to search for new ways to add value to employee benefit programs, multi-carrier (or “choice”) communications have gained popularity. MetLife joined with two other insurance companies to offer group auto insurance. While each of the insurance companies proposed creative for this joint campaign, MKP was selected to develop direct mail, email, landing pages and banner ads, which were used to target the employees of the institutional clients of MetLife, Travelers and Liberty Mutual. The bold, yet simple creative approach, using a driving-related graphic theme, highlighted the advantages of the insurance offer.
