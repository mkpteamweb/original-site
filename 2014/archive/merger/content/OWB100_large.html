OneWest Bank/First Federal/LaJolla Bank Conversion Communications
  • Previous

  • Next
OneWest Bank, a $14B bank formed in 2009 from the failed Indy Mac Bank, subsequently acquired two failed southern California banks, First Federal Bank of California and LaJolla Bank in FDIC-assisted transactions. OneWest engaged MKP to manage the planning, development and execution of conversion communications to 125,000 affected consumer and business customers. Communications needed to reassure customers about the security and stability of OneWest Bank and its commitment to community values and personal service. MKP’s theme, “Building Southern California’s hometown bank, One Person at a Time®,” reinforced their brand promise. Materials were authentic, radiating optimism and reinforcing the warmth and personal communication style of the OneWest brand. The package included a personalized letter with a customized account listing, the transition/welcome brochure and the requisite consolidated disclosure booklet.
