First Citizens Bank/Venture Bank Systems Conversion Communications
  • Previous
  • Index
  • Next
Venture was the first of several FDIC-assisted acquisitions made by this $21 billion North Carolina-based bank with 380 branches in 9 states. Its purchase of the 18 Venture branches in California was an out-of-market expansion, so Venture customers were unfamiliar with First Citizens. Customer communications had to reassure them that their money was safe and that they should feel confident about banking with First Citizens. The headline on the brochures, “the strength to offer you more,” squarely addressed the concerns of the acquired customers by reinforcing a key advantage of banking with First Citizens. MKP’s communications approach was simple, streamlined and straightforward, delivering the professional, clean and crisp style of the First Citizens brand. Consumer and business packages included a personalized letter with an account listing, a conversion brochure and a consolidated disclosure booklet.
