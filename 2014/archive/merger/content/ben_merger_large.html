Beneficial FMS Merger

When Beneficial, the oldest and largest bank headquartered in Philadelphia, acquired Farmers and Mechanics Bank, which served small towns and rural areas in neighboring New Jersey, the communication challenge was to overcome the negative perception that Beneficial was a “big city bank” and reassure customers that Beneficial would retain a strong connection to its customers and a commitment to community banking. MKP developed a merger communications package introducing “new opportunities with the same hometown touch.”
