Sovereign Bank, a large northeast regional bank, expanded into the metro New York market through the acquisition of Independence Community Bank (ICB), a well-established institution with deep roots in many of the areaâ€™s unique neighborhoods. MKP created an oversized merger communication package, with a personalized letter, customized account listing and detailed transition brochure. Photographs of real customers and employees brought the communications to life.
