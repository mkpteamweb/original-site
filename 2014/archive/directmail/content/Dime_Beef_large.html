Dime Savings Bank of Williamsburgh

This long-established community bank in New York sought MKP’s help to generate qualified appointments with attorneys in the area. As an incentive for meeting with a Dime specialist, prospects were offered a $100 gift certificate to a well-known local steakhouse, which, like Dime, has a long tradition serving the community. The provocative envelope teaser “Does Your Bank Deliver the Beef?” and the letter headline “A Rare Opportunity” tie back to the steakhouse theme. To ensure that the mail package would stand out in the mailbox, MKP used a brown envelope, emulating the butcher/steakhouse flavor we hoped to convey in our messaging.
