As an important business strategy to increase customer retention and acquisition, NewAlliance introduced a debit card cash rewards program. MKP drove the strategic positioning for this program, and created a wide range of in-branch materials to saturate the sales environment and generate excitement, along with supporting direct mail and advertising. A common theme and design throughout helped explain the program, in which personal and business checking customers earn “automatic” “cash rewards” “every time” they use their NewAlliance debit card for purchases. Rather than a specific name for the rewards program, MKP created an identifier and unique font – “something extra” – to demonstrate the bank's value proposition.
