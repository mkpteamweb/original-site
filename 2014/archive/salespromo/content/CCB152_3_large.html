Chevy Chase Bank Checking Acquisition Promotion

To motivate prospects and non-checking customers to open checking accounts, Chevy Chase Bank offered an attractive three-day getaway as an incentive. The campaign included a refer-a-friend component, and consisted of numerous branch merchandising elements, electronic elements (emails, landing pages, banner ads, etc.), direct mail, newspaper and radio advertising. To overcome the inherent challenges in acquiring new checking accounts, MKP created a unified promotion with unexpected beach imagery to attract attention and associate the bank with immediate and positive gratification.
