The Dime Savings Bank of Williamsburgh Prime Dime promotion

Key goals for this community bank were to deepen relationships, acquire new customers and build checking deposits. A new tiered-interest checking product, Prime Dime Checking, was positioned as the core of a relationship, which offered premium service and exclusive rate offers on CDs, Money Market accounts and loans. MKP created a fully-integrated marketing campaign on the themes of “Get Rewarded Today” and “Start a Rewarding Relationship” with local print and radio advertising, branch posters, banners and statement inserts. Direct mail targeted prospects and showed the nearest Dime branch location. The campaign was an overwhelming success in attracting new checking dollars.
